Since 1985 · Three Generations

The Atelier

Payal Jewellers began as a single workbench in the lanes of Dhar, where our founder hand-shaped wedding bangles by lamplight. Four decades on, that same workbench still sits at the heart of our store — every kada, chain and mangalsutra we sell is designed, cast and finished by goldsmiths who trained under that very bench.

We work only in BIS hallmarked 22k gold, weigh every piece in front of you, and keep no design locked away — if you can describe it, we can make it. This is jewellery built the old way, for the families who'll wear it the longest.
